COST CENTER POLICY:
Best practice is to keep cost_center_id unique but not overloaded as the only carrier of project meaning.
Codes like CC-BARNES-200-09, CC-KUM-600-12 and CC-KOKE-600-05 are acceptable as operational unique IDs if the model also stores:
- entity_code
- project_id
- cost_center_group
- category_id
- subcategory_id

Avoid excessive disaggregation by using rollups/groupings. The report layer should group equivalent cost center groups across projects.

REAL ESTATE SALES POLICY:
For apartment sales, the movement_type list must include lifecycle/reversal events:
- VENTA_PROMESA
- VENTA_ESCRITURA
- PAGO_PIE
- CUOTA_PIE
- DESISTIMIENTO
- REEMBOLSO_PIE
- PAGO_HIPOTECARIO
- DESEMBOLSO_HIPOTECARIO
- CAMBIO_UNIDAD
- REVERSA_VENTA

Do not delete the original sale when there is a desistimiento. Register the reversal/desistimiento and the refund/payment movement separately.
